About Us

Manalil Mini Super Market Fruit Stall, Bakery, Juice Centre,Wholesale and Retail.

Welcome to Manalil Supermarket, a long-standing establishment rooted in the heart of Chennithala Panchayath, Mavelikara. With a rich history dating back several decades, our family-owned business has been serving the community with dedication and excellence.

Our journey began with a humble rice mill and related business, which gradually expanded to encompass matchstick production, oil sales, and a hardware shop. In 1999, George Thomas (Reji), the youngest son of G. George, had a vision and established a small shop in the iconic Mathen Memorial Buildings. With an initial capital of Rs. 50,000, this marked the inception of our supermarket.

Over the years, through the relentless hard work and commitment of George Thomas and his spouse, Ivy Thomas, our establishment has evolved into a thriving mini supermarket. Today, we proudly operate a bustling store that competes with industry peers and tackles the challenges of a dynamic market.

At Manalil Supermarket, we hold our customers in the highest regard. We believe that our success lies in nourishing, satisfying, and delighting our valued patrons. With this principle at the core of our operations, we are committed to offering an extensive range of quality products at affordable prices, ensuring an unmatched level of customer satisfaction.

Our team strives to create a shopping experience that goes beyond mere transactions. Frequently Asked Questions

The below questions are frequently asked by our customers through phone and other sourses. Customer is the king, so we are ready to resolve any doubts regarding our products and services at any time.It is our responsibility to provide seamless support regarding out services.

  • What kind of product do we have?

    We have all type of FMCG and stationary items. Variety of packet food items are also sold. Not only that but also a small fruit stall, juice centre and a backery section is also a part of our services.

  • Can anyone purchse in the wholesale rate?

    Yes. We provide 90% of our goods in wholesale rate. Anyone can purchase wholesale goods. We welcome daily as well as timely customeres. And retail customers who purchase more goods will be charged only wholesale rates.

  • What is the discount that allowed to customers?

    Assured discount on all purchase(FMCG Products). "The more you buy the more you save".In seasonal times like school reopening, christmas ,onam etc.. percentage wise discounts are also provided.

  • How well can we trust the quality of the products purchase?

    Every product that you buy from our shop is guarinteed. If you are not satisfied with the quality product you will get replaced eventhough the seal of the product is broken, especially in the case of food products.

  • Is door to door delivery available in your shop, Home delivery possible?

    Yes, home delivery is provided. Please call and place order so that our delivery boy will deliver it in time. Please note delivery charges are calculated according to the kilometes. Also, customers can order products through phone or by mail so that we will get the bill ready by the time you arrive in shop.
